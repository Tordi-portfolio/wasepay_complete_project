Customer Service

Welcome to WasePay’s Customer Service page. We are here to assist you with your questions, concerns, or feedback. Whether you're a new investor or a long-time user, we value your satisfaction and trust in our platform.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I invest on WasePay?

Log in to your dashboard, select your investment amount (minimum $100), and follow the on-screen prompts to confirm your transaction.

2. When will I receive my 50% return?

Returns are credited after your selected investment duration—this is typically 7, 14, or 30 days depending on your plan.

3. How do I withdraw my earnings?

Go to your dashboard and click the "Withdraw" button. Ensure your wallet or bank information is correct. Withdrawals take 24–72 business hours to process.

4. I made a mistake during investment. Can I get a refund?

All investments are final. Refunds are only available in cases of verified system error. Please contact support if you're unsure.

5. How do I reach customer service?

You can use the contact form below or email us directly at wasepay@gmail.com.
